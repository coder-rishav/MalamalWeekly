Payment Gateway Management Tips:
  • Test Mode: Use test credentials for development and testing. No real money is processed.
  • Live Mode: Switch to live credentials when ready for production. Real transactions will be processed.
  • Display Order: Lower numbers appear first to users when selecting payment method.
